Four in a row

Four in a row is a strategic game for two people resembling luffar chess. Every other time, the participants put a tray in the game. He who first gets four of his pieces in a row (horizontal, vertical or diagonal) wins. Since tiles are always supplied while the surface is limited, the game ends when there is no room for more tiles. It has been demonstrated that in a match in Four in a row, the one who puts first, if he uses the right strategy, wins, whatever the opponent puts his tiles
